About Company Page


Founded in 2011 by a group of UCF alumni located in Orlando, FL, Knight Digital Marketing has grown to become a trusted source for companies to expand and grow their digital marketing footprint at a reasonable price.


What originally started as an idea by a group of friends working on a business class project in early 2009, Knight Digital Marketing has become a reliable source for companies to expand their online presence. Today, with four locations in total, more than 75,000 businesses trust Knight Digital Marketing to create the content to ensure substantial company growth.
